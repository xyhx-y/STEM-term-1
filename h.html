Radon

Radon is a chemical element with the symbol 'Rn' and atomic number 86. It is a radioactive noble gas that belongs to Group 18 (or 8A) of the periodic table. Radon is a naturally occurring gas produced by the radioactive decay of uranium and thorium in the Earth's crust.It has several isotopes, including radon-222, radon-220, and radon-219. Radon-222 is the most common and stable isotope.
