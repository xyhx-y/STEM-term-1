Reactive Non-Metals

O

Oxygen is a highly reactive non-metal. It is the most abundant element in the Earth's crust and plays a vital role in various biological and chemical processes.

F

Fluorine is the most reactive non-metal. It is highly toxic and reacts vigorously with almost all other elements.

Cl

Chlorine is a greenish-yellow gas that is highly reactive. It is commonly used in disinfectants, bleaches, and as a water treatment agent.

N

Nitrogen is a non-metal that makes up about 78% of the Earth's atmosphere. It is an essential element for all living organisms and is commonly used in fertilizers.
